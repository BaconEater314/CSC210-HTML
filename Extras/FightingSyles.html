Additional Fighting Styles

Pointblank Shot

You no longer have disadvantage on ranged attacks within melee range of enemy creatures.

Double Barrel Master

You can wield the Lightning Shotblaster with one hand, but you cannot have one in each hand.

Cowboy

You can wield a Lightning Revolver in one hand and one non-two-handed weapon in the other. Burst Fire no longer results in you landing prone.

Strong Arsenal

For Firearms you have a Weapon Mastery for, you may instead use Strength for ranged weapon attacks with that weapon.
