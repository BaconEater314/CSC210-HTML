Dress with Pockets

Magic Equipment, Uncommon

It has pockets! This dress provides a base AC of 12. It has two pockets on either side that are extra-dimensional spaces that can hold up to 50 lbs each. This dress can change its appearance once per long rest, but cannot be overtly fancy.

Curse: It has pockets. If anyone says they like your dress, you are obligated to say some form of “Thanks, it has pockets,” and show them the pockets.
