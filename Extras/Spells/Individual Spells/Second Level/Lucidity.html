Lucidity

2nd Level Carian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You touch a creature, clearing its mind, removing any minor madness effects, and removing the need for sleep for the day.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature within 10 feet for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
