Black Flame Blade

2nd Level Black Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (a weapon wielded by a creature)

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You touch a target weapon, enchanting it with black flames. All attacks made with this weapon deal an additional 1d10 black flame damage on a hit.

If you lose concentration on this spell before 1 minute has passed, the effects linger until the next successful hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Druid, Cleric, Paladin, Ranger
