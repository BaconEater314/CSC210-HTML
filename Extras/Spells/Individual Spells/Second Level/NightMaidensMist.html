Night Maiden’s Mist

(level) Level (school)

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60ft

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You summon a cloud of life-sapping energy in a 10-foot radius sphere within range. Creatures that enter this cloud for the first time, start or end their turn in it, take 1d6 force damage.

As a bonus action on your turn, you can move the cloud up to 15 feet.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6, and the radius increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
