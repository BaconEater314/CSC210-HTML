Swarm of Flies

2nd Level Blood Oath Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60ft

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You summon a cloud of bloody flies in a 10-foot radius sphere within range. Creatures that enter this cloud for the first time or end their turn in it take 1d6 piercing damage. Creatures gain blood loss buildup equal to half the damage they take.

As a bonus action on your turn you can move the cloud up to 15 feet.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6, and the radius increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Ranger
