Magic Fortification

2nd Level Two Fingers Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute

You touch a creature, blessing it with magic resistance. The target has resistance to force damage for the duration.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target an additional creature within 10 feet for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
