Discus of Light

2nd Level Golden Order Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (30ft Line)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a small discus of light and throw it in a 5ft wide, 30ft long line. The discus the returns to your hand, passing the same way it came. Whenever the discus passes through a creature it must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 3d6 radiant damage on fail or half as much on success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
