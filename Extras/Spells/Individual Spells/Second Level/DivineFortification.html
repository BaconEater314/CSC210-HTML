Divine Fortification

2nd Level Two Fingers Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute

You touch a creature, granting it divine protection. The target creature gains resistance to either necrotic or radiant damage.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target an additional creature within 10 feet for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Ranger
