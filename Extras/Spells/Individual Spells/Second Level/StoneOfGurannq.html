Stone of Gurranq

2nd Level Beastial Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You grab a large chunk of earth and hurl it at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack roll, dealing 4d8 bludgeoning damage on a hit. When hit, a creature must make a Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Ranger
