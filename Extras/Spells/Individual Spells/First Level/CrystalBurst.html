Crystal Burst

1st Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (20ft cone)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a large barrage of small glintstone shards in a 20ft cone, hitting the first creatures that come in contact with and not affecting creatures behind them. Affected creatures inside the area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d8 force damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. Creatures within 5 feet of you when you cast this spell make the save with disadvantage.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
