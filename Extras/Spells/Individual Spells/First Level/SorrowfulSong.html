Sorrowful Song

1st Level Enchantment

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60 Feet

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You target a creature within range that can hear you. The target must make a Charisma saving throw or suffer disadvantage on either physical or spell attack rolls. A target can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns, ending it on a success. A creature automatically succeeds if it can no longer hear you.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target an additional creature within range.

Spell Lists: Bard
