Gravity Well

(level) Level (school)

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60 Feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You fire a small, dark purple projectile at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack dealing 2d8 force damage on a hit. Regardless of the outcome, you can force the target to make a Strength saving throw, pulling them 10 feet closer on a failure as the projectile pulls them towards it.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 and the pull distance by 5 feet for each slot level above 1st.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
