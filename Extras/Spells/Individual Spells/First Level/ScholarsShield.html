Scholar’s Shield

1st Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (A shield wielded by a creature)

Duration: 1 hour

You touch a shield wielded by a creature. The user of that shield increases their AC by one and reduces all magic damage taken by 1d6 while using it. If you reduce the damage to 0, you take no damage.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you reduce the damage taken from magic by an additional 1d6 for each spell slot above 1st.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
