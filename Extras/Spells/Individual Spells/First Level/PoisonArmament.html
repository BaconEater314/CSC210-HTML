Poison Armament

1st Level Servants of Rot Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: touch

Components: V, S, M (a weapon wielded by a creature)

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You touch a target weapon, enchanting it with poison. All attacks made with this weapon deal an additional 1d6 poison damage on a hit. If you lose concentration on this spell before 1 minute has passed, the effects linger until the next successful hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 per spell slot above 1st level.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
