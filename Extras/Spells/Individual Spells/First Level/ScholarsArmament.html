Scholar’s Armament

(level) Level (school)

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (A melee weapon wielded by someone)

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You touch a target weapon, enchanting it with magical energy. All attacks made with this weapon deal an additional 1d6 force damage on a hit. If you lose concentration on this spell before 1 minute has passed, the effects linger until the next successful hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 per spell slot above 1st level.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
