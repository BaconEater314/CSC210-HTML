Beastial Sling

First Level Beastial Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: self (15ft cone)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You fling sharp rocks at high velocity in front of you, embedding them in creatures in a 15ft cone. All creature in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d6 piercing damage on a fail or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Ranger
