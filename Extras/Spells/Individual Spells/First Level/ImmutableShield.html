Immutable Shield

1st Level Golden Order Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (A shield wielded by a creature)

Duration: 1 hour

You touch a shield wielded by a creature, enchanting it with divine protection. The user of that shield increases their AC by 1 and reduces physical, radiant, necrotic, and force damage taken by 1d6 while using it. If you reduce the damage to 0, you take no damage.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you reduce the damage taken from magic by an additional 1d6 for each spell slot above 1st.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
