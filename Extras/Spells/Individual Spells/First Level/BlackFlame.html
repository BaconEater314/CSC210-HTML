Black Flame

First Level Black Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60 Feet

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Creating a large ball of black flames in your hand, you throw it at a point within range. All creatures within 10 feet must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d8 black fire damage on a fail, or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 per spell slot above 1st level.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
