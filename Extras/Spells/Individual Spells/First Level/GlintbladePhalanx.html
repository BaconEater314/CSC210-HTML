Glintblade Phanlanx

1st Level Carian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute

You summon a small group of 5 glinstone blades that float about your head, which last for 1 minute. Whenever a creature of your choice walks within 15 feet of you, all 5 blades fire at it. Target must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 5d4 force damage on a success, or half as much on a failure.

Creatures have disadvantage on this saving throw if it triggers immediately after you cast this spell.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you summon two additional blades (dealing an additional 2d4) for each spell slot above 1st.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
