Spell Name

1st Level Two Fingers Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You touch a creature with a cleansing hand, removing the poisoned condition from them. Target creature has advantage on Constitution saving throws against being poisoned for the next hour.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you may target an additional creature within 10 feet for each slot level above 1st.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger
