Founding Rain of Stars

6th Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60ft

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You conjure a small number of stars which fly into the air above a point within range, creating a large cloud which rains stars which remains for the duration. All creatures in a 20ft sphere that start their turn under the cloud or enter it for the first time must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d6 force damage on a failure or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 6th.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
