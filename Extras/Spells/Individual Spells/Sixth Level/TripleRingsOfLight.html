Triple Rings of Light

6th Level Golden Order Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (30ft Cone)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You create three large rings of light and toss them out, striking enemies they pass through. All creatures in a 30ft cone must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 6d6 radiant damage on a fail, or half as much on a success. The rings then return to you, forcing the save against creatures in the cone, still dealing 6d6 radiant damage.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 6th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
