Lansseax's Glaive

6th Level Dragon Cult Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (60ft Cone)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Channeling ancient dragon magic, you create a massive glaive and sweep it, sending red lightning throughout a 60ft cone. All creatures in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 10d6 AD Lightning damage on a fail or half as much on a success. Creatures adjacent to you when you cast this spell have disadvantage on the saving throw.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 6th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
