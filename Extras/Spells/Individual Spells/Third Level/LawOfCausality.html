Law of Casualty

3rd Level Golden Order Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You touch a willing creature, granting it a damage-storing blessing. Any time a creature takes damage, you gain one casualty stack. When this spell ends, the blessed creature expels the stored damage. All creatures within 10 feet must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d6 radiant damage per casualty stack, or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the target starts with an additional 2 casualty stacks per spell slot above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
