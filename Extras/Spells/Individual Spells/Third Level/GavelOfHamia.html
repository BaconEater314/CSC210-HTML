Gavel of Hamia

3rd Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (10ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You conjure a massive hammer and slam it into the ground, creating a 10ft radius shockwave around you. All creatures in the area (except you) must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d6 force damage on a failure, or half as much on a success.

If you benefit from extra attack, you may cast this spell again on your turn for each attack without expending a spell slot.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
