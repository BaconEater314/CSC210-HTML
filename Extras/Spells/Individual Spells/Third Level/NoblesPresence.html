Noble's Presence

3rd Level Black Flame Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (10ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You thrust out your belly with gusto, unleashing a shockwave. All creatures within 10 feet must make a Strength saving throw. Creatures that fail take 4d6 bludgeoning and 4d6 black fire damage, are pushed 10 feet away, and are knocked prone. Creatures that succeed take half as much damage and are only pushed 5 feet away.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the black fire damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.When you cast this spell using a spell slot of (level+1) level or higher, the damage increases by (damage die) per spell slot above (level) level.

Spell Lists: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Warlock
