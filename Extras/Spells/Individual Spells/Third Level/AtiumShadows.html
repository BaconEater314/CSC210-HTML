Atium Shadows

3rd Level Chronomancy

Casting Time: Bonus Action

Range: Self

Components: S, M (A small ball of rare metal that you swallow when you cast this spell)

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

Target a creature that you can see within 120 feet that must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failure, you gain insight into how it will take its turn, including where it plans to move, who it will attack, what spell it will cast, etc, through blue shadows that reveal its movement. You have advantage on attack rolls against the target, and it has disadvantage on saving throws against your spells. It has disadvantage on attack rolls against you, and you have advantage on saving throws against their effects.

The target may change their plans before their turn arrives based on circumstances, such as moving around difficult terrain that appeared via a spell. When this happens, you are notified of this change as long as you are still concentrating on this spell.

A creature can repeat the save at the end of its turn (ignoring the disadvantage from this spell), ending the effect on a success. On a failure, you gain insight of their next turn.

Spell Lists: Wizard, Cleric (Time Domain)
