Gurranq’s Beast Claw

3rd Level Beastial Incantation

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Self(20ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You slam a clawed hand into the ground and rip up the ground around you. All creatures within 20 feet of you must make a Strength saving throw, taking 8d6 slashing damage on a fail and are knocked prone, or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Ranger
