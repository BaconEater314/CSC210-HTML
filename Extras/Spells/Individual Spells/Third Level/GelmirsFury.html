Gelmir’s Fury

3rd Level Magma Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (15ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

Channeling deep magma beneath the earth, you slam your staff into the ground, sending magma hurtling in the air around you. All creatures in a 15ft radius sphere centered on you (excluding you) must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d8 fire damage on a fail, or half as much on a success. The area in the sphere becomes difficult terrain until the end of your next turn.

As an action on subsequent turns while concentrating, you can create this effect again.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 3rd.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
