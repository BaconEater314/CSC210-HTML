Radagon’s Rings of Light

5th Level Golden Order Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self (20ft Radius)

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You create a ring of light above your head and expanding around you. All creatures within 20 feet of you must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 8d8 radiant damage on a fail or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 5th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
