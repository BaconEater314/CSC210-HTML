Comet Azur

8th Level Glintstone Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self(120ft Line)

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You charge up powerful magics, unleashing a blast of powerful glintstone energy in a 5ft wide, 120ft long line. All creatures that start their turn in the line, or enter it for the first time, must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 10d8 force damage on failure, or half as much on a success.

While concentrating on this spell, you can take an action on your turn to recast this spell in the same area without expending a spell slot, otherwise, it ends. Additionally, you cannot move while casting this spell.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell at 9th level, you gain the ability to rotate this spell up to 90 degrees when you recast this spell on your turn. The damage also increases by 2d8.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard.
