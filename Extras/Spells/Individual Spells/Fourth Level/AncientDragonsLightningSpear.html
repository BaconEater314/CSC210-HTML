Ancient Dragon’s Lightning Spear

4th Level Dragon Cult Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

Drawing on ancient draconic lightning, you create a massive lightning spear and slam it into the ground. All creatures adjacent to you and in four 5-foot-wide, 15-foot-long lines that are 90 degrees from each other must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 8d6 AD lightning damage on a fail or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid
