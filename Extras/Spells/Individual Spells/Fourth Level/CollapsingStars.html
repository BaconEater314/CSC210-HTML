Collapsing Stars

4th Level Gravity Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 90ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You create 9 gravity projectiles and launch them at foes within range. You can hurl them at one target or several. Make 9 spell attack rolls, dealing 1d8 force damage on a hit.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you create an additional projectile for every spell slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
