Gravitational Missile

4th Level Gravity Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 30ft

Components: V, S

Duration: 1 minute (Concentration)

You create a large gravity well at a point within range, pulling creatures toward its center. When a creature that starts its turn within 15 feet of the gravity well, which is difficult terrain, it must make a Strength saving throw, being pulled 10 feet closer on a failure or 5 feet on a success.

When a creature starts its turn or becomes adjacent to the well for the first time, it takes 2d10 force damage.

When you end concentration, all creatures within 10 feet of the well must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d10 force damage on a failure or half as much on a success.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the final explosion damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
