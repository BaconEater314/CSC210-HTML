Litany of Proper Death

4th Level Golden Order Incantation

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: 60ft

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You bless the ground within range, blasting a 20ft radius sphere with holy energy. All creatures of your choice in the area must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 8d6 radiant damage on a fail, or half as much on a success. Targets that fail are also blinded.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists: Cleric, Druid, Paladin
