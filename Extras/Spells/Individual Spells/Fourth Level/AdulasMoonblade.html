Adula’s Moonblade

4th Level Carian Sorcery

Casting Time: 1 Action

Range: Self

Components: V, S

Duration: Instantaneous

You summon a massive blade at the end of your staff that slices through objects and foes alike. Make a melee spell attack roll against two targets, dealing 4d6 + your spellcasting modifier Force damage on a hit.

All creatures in a 15ft wide, 25 ft long line when you swing must make a Dexterity Saving throw, taking 2d6 force damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. Creatures hit by this spell’s melee attack have disadvantage on the saving throw.

If you benefit from extra attack or have another action on your turn, you may swing the magic blade again on your turn without expending a spell slot.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for both effects for each slot level above 4th.

Spell Lists: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
