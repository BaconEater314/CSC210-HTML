Human

The Human, the most common race in Ardonia. They are known for their adaptability and ambition, making them well-suited to thrive in diverse environments across the realm.

Humans are hardy and resilient, and often surprise others with their will to persevere.

Creature Type: You are a Humanoid.

Size: You are Medium or Small. You select your size when you choose this race.

Speed: Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and any other language of your choice.

Human Weapon Training: You gain proficiency with the following weapons:

Indomitable Human Spirit: Once per day, when are reduced to 0 hit points (but are not killed outright), you can choose to instead drop to 1 hit point.

You also gain immunity to all damage until the start of your next turn. However, when combat ends you gain 2 levels of exhaustion.

Feat: You gain one feat of your choice.
