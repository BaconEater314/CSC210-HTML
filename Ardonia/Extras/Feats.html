Extra Feats

Dense Body

Prerequisite: Con save Proficiency, Tough feat, Durable feat, or Vigor of the Hill Giants feat.

Your flesh is thicker, your bones denser than the average person. You gain the following benefits:

  • Your Constituion or Strength score increases by 1 (maximum of 20).
  • Your Critical Injury Threshold increaes by 3.
  • When ever you are critically hit, you reduce the damage by your Constitution modifier (minimum 1) after applying resistances.

Song Weaver

Prerequisite: Ardoni

You've had extensive practice wielding the Songs of War all your life. This pracitce helps you use them more powerfully, and more often. You gain the following benefits:

  • Increase your Song casting ability score (Constituion, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma) by 1 (maximum of 20).
  • You can use the Songs of War one additional time per day.
  • Once per day, you regain half a Song's uses on a short rest.
