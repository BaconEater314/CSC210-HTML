(Race)

The Magnorite, a typically sullen, reclusive, and violent race (with rare exceptions) made of the earth and molten rock. Their skin is formed of rock and cracked in places, forming unique patterns much like the Ardoni, which reveal their lava-like blood. It is unclear if these cracks are unhealed wounds or genetic markings. Magnorites are strong, though terrible builders, often occupying patchwork buildings or old, stolen houses and caves.

Typically residing in the rocky and harsh lands in the South of Ardonia, Magnorites form secluded societies. In these societies, strength is determined by the number of skulls claimed from defeated foes. The more the skulls, the stronger the Magnorite.

Creature Type: Humanoid

Size: Your size is Medium. Magnorites usually range from 6ft to 8ft tall.

Age: Magnorites mature at age 3 and live up to 30 years.

Speed: Definition

Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and Ignan.

Magma Walker: Magnorites have resistance to fire damage and can walk in and on lava without taking fire damage.

Powerful Build: Magnorites double their push/drag/lift and carry capacity.

Heavy Weight: Magnorites have advantage on saving throws against effects that would forcefully move them. Even when forcefully moved, they are only moved half the distance. Magnorites also cannot swim and just sink.

Powerful Punches: Magnorites do 1d6 Bludgeoning damage with unarmed strikes.

Natural Armor Magnorites gain a +1 to AC while not wearing armor. You can use a shield and still gain this benefit.

Creatures of Stone Magnorites do not need to sleep, eat, or drink, but do require rest.
