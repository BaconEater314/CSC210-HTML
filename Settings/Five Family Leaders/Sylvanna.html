Sylvana Nightblade (Azne)

Nobody really knows how or why she got here. Word on the street is, she’s well over 200, making her older than Dante De Luca, but she doesn't look a day over 30. She’s fanatically devout to her cause and openly bears strange tattoos that pulsate and glow in strange and seemingly painful ways. She’ll only answer to her Draconic name, though her true name is public knowledge following her first arrest 18 years ago.

Personality Traits My tattoos burn like molten lava. I love it.
