Bouncer

This background is designed for the Upside setting.

Skills: Athletics, Intimidation

Tools: N/A

Languages: N/A

Equipment: A set of Dark Common Clothes, a wallet containing 20 GP in bills, a pair of Brass Knuckles, and a handful of confiscated fake IDs

Built Like a Brick

You are built like a brick, a more than average-sized for your race, and are adept at blocking other creatures. When standing in a doorway, creatures of your size or smaller are unable to pass through without your permission. You have advantage on Athletics checks against creatures attempting to push you.

Your Critical Injury Threshold also increases by 1.
