Mafioso

This background is designed for the Upside setting.

Skills: Intimidation, Sleight of Hand

Tools: Thieves' Tools

Languages: One of your choice, which is associated with the organization you are part of

Equipment: A set of Dark Common Clothes, a Wallet containing 15 GP in bills, a Set of thieves' tools, a simple weapon with which you are proficient, and a contact book with the names of several other made men in your organization.

Made Man

You have earned the respect and membership of a major criminal element in the city. Other made men of your organization will recognize you as being made to the organization and will not be hostile to you, though they may not trust your allies if they are not made themselves.
