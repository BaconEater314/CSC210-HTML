Warrior of the Blue Moon

Prerequisite: Goblin

Warriors of the Blue Moon are a group of Goblin Warriors that defended the Blue Moon Orchard and Vineyard. They were instrumental during the Battle of Rivermere, coming to the defense of the town and fighting off the Mazetecan armies, allowing the people to escape. Though mostly destroyed and disbanded after the Battle, few warriors still exist and are known for their valor and skill in battle.

This background is designed for the Rise of an Empire and Grimm Campaign settings.

Skills: Perception, Intimidation

Damage Resistances: Radiant

Equipment: A broken weapon from the Battle of Rivermere, a memento of someone who helped you, a set of common clothes, and a pouch containing 20 GP.

Blue Moon

You can cast Moonbeam once per day with expending a spell slot, using your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma as your spell-casting modifier. While concentrating on this spell, you can reposition it as a bonus action.

Battle Valor.

You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Additionally, you are widely known as a hero. People will help you to the best of their ability if they can, often going out of their way to help even if it might be detrimental to them.
