Monk: Way of the Gun Fu

A strange breed of martial artists, trained in the arts of pistols. The Gun Fu monks excel at blasting away and making impossible shots around corners.

This subclass is designed for the Upside setting.

Firearm Proficiency

When you choose this archetype, you gain proficiency with simple firearms, and they count as Monk weapons for you.

Flurry of Bolts

Additionally, at 3rd level, when you use your Flurry of Blows Ki move, you can replace your unarmed strikes with ranged Firearm attacks.

Curve the Bullet

Starting at 6th level, you can extend your Ki to make your shots curve around corners. When you make a ranged attack roll with a firearm, you can spend 1 ki point to ignore half or three-quarters cover. You can instead spend 2 ki points to hit creatures behind total cover, provided you know their whereabouts and the bolt has a path to reach them.
