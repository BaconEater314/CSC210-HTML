Druid: Circle of the Sewer Rats

This subclass is designed for the Upiside setting.

Circle Spells

Your mystical connection to the Sewers infuses you with the ability to cast certain spells. At 2nd, 3rd, 5th, 7th, and 9th level, you gain access to circle spells.

Once you gain access to a circle spell, you always have it prepared, and it doesn't count against the number of spells you can prepare each day. If you gain access to a spell that doesn't appear on the druid spell list, the spell is nonetheless a druid spell for you.

Circle of the Sewer Rats Spells
Druid Level Spells
1st Entangle, Ray of Sickness
3rd Enlarge/Reduce, Protection from Poison
5th Gaseous Form, Stinking Cloud
7th Blight, Giant Insect
9th Cloudkill, Contagion

Swarm Shape

When you choose this circle at 2nd level, when you Wildshape you can now turn into swarms of creatures of appropriate CR of your Wildshape table. While Wildshaped as a swarm, you can cast your circle spells.
